a Gift for You

She has an aura about her
A thorn bush jaw
to compliment her rose petal lips
Her teeth like piano keys
Grinding melodies of soothing lullabies
For those that enjoy her comfort
Although waking up late with two hours of sleep
Can sometimes make it difficult to negotiate the weight
of her eyelids
When her eyebrows raise
The wrinkles on her forehead gave her two extra smiles
She has an origami heart that can be unfolded
by the slightest of words
And a welcoming soul
That always leaves the door open
She is always ready to smile as if she lived
In a world filled with yellow lights
Yielding to those whose lives she wants to impact.
